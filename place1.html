📍 South Gate (Minglun Campus)

📜 History: The South Gate of Henan University was completed in 1936. The inscription "Henan University" on the gate was written by Dr. Sun Yat-sen. It has welcomed countless students and symbolizes the spirit of knowledge and patriotism.

🔍 Next clue: Walk straight ahead from the gate. You will see a grand building with a traditional Chinese roof and Western columns. It once hosted important events during the war. Find the Grand Auditorium.
