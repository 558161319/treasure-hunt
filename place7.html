📍 7. Yifu New Library

📜 Introduction: The Yifu Library (New Building) of Henan University was funded and constructed by Mr. Shao Yifu. The architectural style is a blend of Chinese and Western elements, complementing the surrounding historical buildings. The library houses a rich collection of books and is well-equipped, featuring functions for reading, collection, and academic exchanges. It is a landmark cultural building on the campus and an important repository of literature and information.
