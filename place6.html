🗿 Iron Pagoda (1049 AD)

📜 History: The Iron Pagoda, built in 1049 during the Northern Song Dynasty, is a 13-story glazed brick pagoda standing 55 meters tall. Despite its name, it is made of dark brown bricks that resemble iron. It has survived floods, earthquakes, and wars, standing as a testament to Kaifeng's glorious past. Henan University students often visit it for inspiration.
