📍 6. Imperial Examination Stone Steles

📜 Introduction: The Imperial Examination Stone Steles of Henan University consists of two ancient stone tablets from the Qing Dynasty. They respectively record the renovation of the Henan Imperial Examination Hall during the Yongzheng period and the reconstruction during the Daoguang period. This place was once the examination venue for the imperial examinations in Henan during the Qing Dynasty, and it was also the venue for the last two imperial examinations at the end of the imperial examination system. It witnessed the end of the thousand-year imperial examination system and holds significant historical value.
