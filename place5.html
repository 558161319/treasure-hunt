📖 Old Library (1980s)

📜 History: Built in the 1980s, the Old Library has been a sanctuary for students seeking knowledge. Its collection includes rare local gazetteers and historical documents. The reading rooms echo with the quiet dedication of generations of scholars.

🗿 Next clue: Leave the library and walk east towards the city wall. You will see a thousand-year-old pagoda made of glazed bricks. It is the Iron Pagoda, a symbol of Kaifeng. Find your final stop there.
