📍 2. Preparatory School Old Gate

📜 Introduction: This is the reconstructed gate of the Henan School for Students Going Abroad to Europe and America within the Minglun Campus of Henan University. It replicates the gate of the predecessor of Henan University in 1912. At that time, the preparatory school was one of the three major bases for studying abroad in the Republic of China, initiating modern higher education in Henan. With the original intention of saving the country through education, it cultivated a large number of talents who studied abroad to serve the country and witnessed the era's trend of the westward spread of knowledge and the pursuit of industrial strength.
